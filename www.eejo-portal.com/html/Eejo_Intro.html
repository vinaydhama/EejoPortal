Welcome to Eejo Swim Portal

Registration

Participants access the registration portal.
Fill in personal details (Name, Contact, School/Club).
Select events and upload required documents.
Complete payment and receive confirmation.
Download registration form or QR code for check-in.

Check-In

Check in at the venue using card or QR code.
Handle corrections if any.
Swimmers receive heat details.
Heat list updated based on check-ins.


Heat Execution

Swimmers arranged in heat order.
Heat loaded and displayed on timer screen.
Referee starts the timer.
Referee applies necessary flags (DQ, AB,DNC).

Stop watch

Timekeepers record finish times.

Event Result Compilation

Final event results generated.
Tentative results displayed online.
Concerns reviewed and final results published.

Result display
